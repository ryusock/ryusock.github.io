Projects

Berkeley's Most Unpredictable Buses

I analyzed which buses around UC Berkeley arrive later than expected by polling public bus data every minute for weeks (more than 45,000 bus trips in total). Delays were calculated based on deviation from published bus prediction times, and compared by stop, route, and time of day. Published in The Daily Californian.

Analyzing Rent Controls in Berkeley

I scraped the City of Berkeley's public database for rent control and parcel information to evaluate which neighborhoods had higher rent prices and compared large landowners.

Visualizing the Women's March

Taking aggregated data of locally-reported counts on demonstrations on Inauguration Day, I mapped the demonstrations by location and sized by area.
